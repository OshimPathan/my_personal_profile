Hi. I'm Oshim Pathan, a software engineer from Devdaha, Rupandehi, Nepal. I'm in my final year of B.Tech in Computer Science at VIT Vellore, India (CGPA: 8.78/10). My focus lies at the intersection of AI, healthcare, and cloud computing.
